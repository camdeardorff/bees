Hello Friends!

Welcome to Bees?.

At the start of this semester I came to realize just how much I enjoy sitting in the caf and doing homework when it is still. That is how this little project started. Giving visibility to just how loud the caf is right now seemed like a worthwhile and beneficial thing to have and to share with everyone. So whether you are looking for some quiet study time or a fun time with friends, Bees? is the place for you.
